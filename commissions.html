Overview

When doing commissions, it is important to set a few rules in order to allow the artist to create the desired piece of art. For instance, traditional art takes longer to complete and it is much more delicate as oppossed to digital art, where there is more freedom.

*Read the following before commissioning

  • All commissions are done under contract
  • Visual References
  • Prices vary depending on size, medium, and movement (ex. Portrait> painting> abstract)
  • Time for completion will also depend on what was previously stated
  • Must be able to pay 50% upfront (Finalize purchase upon completion)
  • Once sketch/concept/draft is approved, there won't be any changes
  • Sales are final
  • No Refunds
