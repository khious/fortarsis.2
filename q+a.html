Q & A

Do you have any art careers?
No, I am mostly self taught even though I took a few art classes during my high school years.
How much are your commissions?
There isn't a set price, it would be difficult to come up with a price model since most commissions vary by canvas size, materials, and other factors such as shipping.
Do you retain rights fo each commission?
Yes, for each work of art, I reserve production rights, as well as the right to exhibit the work. Everything is in the contract upon signing and finalizing.
Who are your biggest incluencers?
As far as surrealism painting, definetly Salvador Dali, a skilled artist best known for the bizzare iamges in his work.
What inspires you to paint?
I am inspired by a lot of things, like the process of painting itself is very relaxing. Creating something new and different using canvas, pigments, and my own imagination.
Additionally, the idea of communication ideas and feelings to other people through my work and seeing my art work resoante with you, the viewer and art enthusiats.
Did you always want to be an artist?
Yes, ever since I was a kid i always loved portraying my emotions and feelings through my creations.
What advice would you give your younger self?
I would tell myself to never stop creating art, work smart, hard and add a dose of self promotion. At one point I completly stopped drawing, and paiting for almost three years. I worked hard and smart but totally oblivious to the self-promotion part. Which resulted in people not knowing my art even existed.
